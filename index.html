British Shorthair Kitten Calculator

Are you unsure what colour your kittens will be? Use this calculator to find out!

As you go through the calculator you will input the colour of the father of the kittens, followed by the colour of the mother.

Each question has a corresponding image, so you can see the colour and pattern. As you input the details, the image at the top will change to represent the cat you are currently creating.

The calculator will ask you about recessive genes as well to find out all of the possible colours the kittens could be. If you are not sure what recessive genes your cats might carry, it is best to assume that they do not carry any. You could also do a calculation for both options and see what the difference is! Most recessive genes can be tested for by sending a simple cheek swab to a lab. Langford Vets are a popular option in the UK.
